Introduction¶

The following project is used to analyse data related to movies . It contains inforamation such as director name, starcast, runtime etc.we analyse data using numpy,pandas and matplotlib.

The following questions have been asked:¶

  • Which movie has highest and lowest revenue?
  • Which movie has highest and lowest budget?
  • Which is the most popular director?
  • Which movie has highest and lowest runtime?
  • What is the average popularity rate in each year?

Conclusions¶

  • Avatar is the movie with highest revenue
  • Shatter Glass is the lowest revenue movie
  • The movie with the highest budget is The Warrior's Way
  • The movie with the lowest budget is Lost & Found
  • Steven Spielberg is the most popular director
  • Carlos has highest runtime
  • Kid's Story has lowest runtime
  • Year 2015 has highest poularity rate

Limitations¶

  • It's not 100 percent guaranteed solution and this formula may or may not work, But it shows us that we have high probability of making high profits if we have similar characteristics as such. If we release a movie with these characteristics, it may or maynot be a hit
  • During cleaning i dropped the rows which had budget and revenue equal to 0 .I converted them to null and dropped them. But it could have been done more effectively.we could have used internet to find the missing values. that would have given more accurate results.
